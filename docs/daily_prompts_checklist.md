# Daily Antigravity Prompts Checklist

Run these exact prompts in your Antigravity chat to operate the job search tool each day.

### 1. Morning Sync (Job Fetching)
**Prompt:**
> "Fetch new job postings from our tracked sources (Greenhouse for Vercel, Lever for Figma)."

### 2. Candidate Evaluation (Scoring)
**Prompt:**
> "Get all pending, unscored job postings. Evaluate each one's fit out of 10 against my resume and save the scores with a short reasoning."
*Note: Make sure your current resume text is either in the chat context or uploaded as a file.*

### 3. Daily Tracking (Follow-ups)
**Prompt:**
> "List all contacts that are due for a follow-up today."

### 4. Ad-hoc Outreach (Drafting)
**Prompt:** 
> "Add a new contact named [Name] who works at [Company]. We are [Relation/Alumni]. Then, draft a short, personalized LinkedIn outreach message for them and save the draft."

### 5. Application Management
**Prompt:**
> "Update my application for the [Role] at [Company] to 'interview_scheduled' and set the next action to 'Technical Screen' due on [Date]."
